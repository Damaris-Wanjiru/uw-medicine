Supporting Valley with the Gift of Time

Volunteers in Action
Care For Our Community Like Family

Every hour you donate and every smile you share with others
can impact someone's life. Consider becoming part of our
Volunteer team and using a few hours of your time and talent
each week to give back to those in our community who need
support. Valley volunteers are goodwill ambassadors,
spreading hope and cheer, providing vital assistance for our
patients and visitors. Whether serving as a greeter at the
Information Desk or escorting visitors, helping in a support
department,or supporting in a clinical area like the Breast
Center, volunteers are a treasured part of our
Valley family, contributing the most important of all
ingredientsâ€”love and concern for people.

Valley has nearly 250 active volunteers who donate approximately 40,000 hours each year in many
different departments and roles throughout the organization. Supplementing the services performed
by our staff, volunteers are an integral part of our healthcare team and valuable contributors who provide
special added touches that make a patient's stay more comfortable.
To show our appreciation for your service, we offer volunteers several benefits, including:

  • Complimentary meal with each shift worked
  • Volunteer recognition events & activities
  • Enrollment fee waived at the fitness center, and a monthly discounted price.
